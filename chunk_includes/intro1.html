Instructions

Please read the following instructions carefully before beginning.

There are two parts to this study: one conducted here and the other to be completed at home over the internet.

    Part 1: At the end of each video, your job is to make guesses about the meanings of the mystery word in the video.

  • You must make at least one guess, but you will be able to provide up to 5 guesses for each video. You may skip your unused guesses by simply clicking the "Continue" link at the bottom of the page.
  • For each guess, please use just a single word, such as "unicorn", rather than either/or responses ("unicorn/find") or phrases or sentences (e.g., "Find the unicorn!").
  • The same mystery word may occur in one or more videos, so you may make the same guesses for one or more videos that you watch.
  • For each guess you make, we want you to rate how confident you are.
  • You will use a scale where a rating of 1 means you are not at all confident in your guess and a rating of 5 means that you are very confident in your guess. You are encouraged to use the full range of the scale when reporting your confidence.
  • When you are finished making your guesses for a video, please look up at the display screen on the wall. The experimenter will wait until everybody is ready before proceeding to the next video.
  • Please do not begin guessing before a video has ended.
  • You will be watching 60 videos altogether, which will take about 1 hour.
